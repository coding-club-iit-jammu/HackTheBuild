About the Event

A two-week Hackathon where students from various institutes across the country will team up to find the solution to the problem statements provided by us.

Undergraduate and Postgraduate students of age group from all across the nation. Team participation with size of 1-5.

=> 11:00 AM (IST), October 2 to 05:00 PM (IST) October 17

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?
Hack the build is absolutely free of cost, so what are you waiting for? Register Now
What are the Problem Statements?
This time we are not providing any particular problem statement, you are free to create anything based on the themes provided

Themes :
  1. Remote Working
  2. Healthcare
  3. Blockchain
  4. Open Innovation
Who can participate?
The hackathon is restricted to students only. All the hackers participating must be students.
Can I participate Individually ?
No, you are required to have atleast 2 and at most 3 members in your team.
How to submit hacks?
Check Submission rules here.
Can i start working now?
You can research about the ideas but the commits should in between hackathon time. Any commit before or after the hackathon can lead to disqualification.
